# Treasury
## usage:
* deposit(address, ammount, token) adds tokens to account trying transfer from
* withdraw(address, ammount, token) tries to take tokens to the address from account
### token - integer field one of:
    1.  Usdt: 0
    2.  Usdc: 1
    3.  Tusd: 2
    4.  Usdn: 3
    5.  Busd: 4
    6.  Husd: 5
    7.  Dai:  6
    8.  Susd: 7
    9.  Usdk: 8
    10. Ust:  9
    11. Musd: 10
    12. Gusd: 11
